What Is a Legal Notice for Recovery of Money?

A legal notice for recovery of money is a formal written demand sent by an advocate to recover unpaid dues.
It informs the recipient that:

A specific amount is legally payable

The failure to pay violates legal or contractual obligations

Legal action will follow if payment is not made within a stipulated time

This notice becomes a crucial document if the dispute later goes to court.
