## üìù Content Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Style:
- **Professional but approachable**
- **EEAT-friendly** (Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- **Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Action** flow
- **Clear, jargon-free language** (except where legal terms necessary)

### Structure Rules:
1. **Lead with user problem**
   - "If someone owes you money and refuses to pay..."

2. **Define the solution clearly**
   - "A legal notice for money recovery is a formal demand..."

3. **Explain the process**
   - "When you send a notice, the following happens..."

4. **Address objections**
   - "Is it mandatory? While not always required..."

5. **Show credibility**
   - "Vakiltech's lawyers have recovered ‚Çπ12.3 Cr for clients..."

6. **Strong CTA**
   - "Start your money recovery notice today ‚Üí"

### SEO Writing Tips:
- Use primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, and 1-2 H2s
- Natural keyword density: 1-2%
