# Defamation Legal Notice Format & Guide

Before you rush to file a criminal case or a civil suit for defamation, there is one powerful step you should take: **Sending a Legal Notice.**

A well-drafted legal notice acts as a "shot across the bow." It warns the offender that you are serious, legally prepared, and willing to take them to court if they don't make amends. In many cases, a strong notice is enough to get the defamatory content removed and an apology issued.

This guide provides a professional format for a defamation legal notice in India and explains how to use it effectively.

---

## Why Send a Legal Notice?

1.  **It shows "Malice" later:** If they ignore your notice and continue defaming you, you can prove to the court that they did it intentionally despite being warned.
2.  **It's Cheaper:** A notice costs a fraction of what a court case costs. If it solves the problem, you save lakhs.
3.  **It clarifies facts:** Sometimes, people defame out of ignorance. A notice puts the correct facts on record.

---

## Essential Elements of a Defamation Notice

To be legally valid and effective, your notice must contain:
1.  **Clear Narrative:** What exactly did they say? When? Where?
2.  **The "Imputation":** How specifically did those words harm your reputation? (e.g., "It lowered my credit in the market," "It caused my marriage proposal to break.")
3.  **Malicious Intent:** Assert that they knew it was false and did it to harm you.
4.  **The Demand (Requisition):** What do you want?
    *   Unconditional Apology?
    *   Removal of post?
    *   Monetary Compensation?
5.  **Deadline:** Give them time to act (usually 7, 10, or 15 days).

## Sample Defamation Legal Notice Format

**WARNING:** This is a general draft. For serious matters, always get it customized and sent by a registered Advocate on their letterhead. A notice from a lawyer carries far more weight than one sent personally.

***

**Ref No:** [Date]
**By Registered Post A.D. / Speed Post**

To,
**[Name of Accused]**
[Address of Accused]
[City, State, Zip]

**SUBJECT: LEGAL NOTICE FOR DEFAMATION**

Sir/Madam,

Under instructions from and on behalf of my client, **Mr./Ms. [Your Name]**, resident of [Your Address], I do hereby serve upon you the following legal notice:

1.  That my client is a respectable citizen holding a high status in society and is currently working as [Your Profession/Designation]. My client has built an impeccable reputation over years of hard work and integrity.

2.  That you, the Noticee, harboring ill-will and malicious intent towards my client, have published/circulated the following false, baseless, and defamatory statements against my client on **[Date]** at **[Platform - e.g., Facebook / Company Meeting / Newspaper]**:
    *   *"[Quote the defamatory statement 1 directly]"*
    *   *"[Quote the defamatory statement 2 directly]"*

3.  That the aforesaid statements are completely false and have been made by you knowing fully well that they are untrue. You have made these allegations solely with the intention to defame, humiliate, and harass my client in the eyes of the public/colleagues/family.

4.  That due to your defamatory acts, my client has suffered immense mental agony, harassment, and loss of reputation. [Mention specific loss if any, e.g., loss of business contract].

5.  That your act constitutes the offense of **Defamation** punishable under **Section 356 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), 2023** (previously Section 500 IPC), which attracts imprisonment of up to two years. It also makes you liable for civil damages.

**I, THEREFORE, CALL UPON YOU through this specific Notice to:**

1.  **IMMEDIATELY WITHDRAW** the said defamatory statements from [Platform/Website].
2.  **TENDER AN UNCONDITIONAL APOLOGY** in writing and publish the same on the same platform where the original defamation was published.
3.  **PAY A SUM OF ₹[Amount]** towards compensation for the mental agony and loss of reputation caused to my client.
4.  **PAY A SUM OF ₹[Amount]** towards the cost of this legal notice.

Within **15 (Fifteen) days** from the receipt of this notice, failing which I have firm instructions from my client to initiate appropriate Civil and Criminal legal proceedings against you in the competent court of law entirely at your risk, cost, and consequences.

Copy retained in my office for future record.

**[Name of Advocate]**
Advocate, [Court Name]
[Signature]

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

*   **Vague Allegations:** Don't just say "He insulted me." Quote the exact words.
*   **Missing Deadline:** Always specify a time limit for them to reply.
*   **Sending via WhatsApp only:** While valid, it's best to send via **Registered Post A.D.** so you have proof of delivery (the Acknowledgment Card) to show the court.
